Each arm is checked as a built-in cantilever (bending, shear, LTB, tip deflection) to the design-layer code. The rated SWL is the lower of rack serviceability and member design. Use the buttons to open the full member-design report for any beam in a new tab.

Ctrl/⌘ + scroll = zoom · drag = pan · wheel = page scroll

Members are coloured by section — same colour means the same section. The deformed shape and amber sway probe use the SLS combination; red loads and member peak forces use ULS. Use the zoom buttons or hold Ctrl/⌘ while scrolling to zoom; a normal wheel movement scrolls the page. Drag to pan, hover a member for its section and peak forces, or click to inspect it in Diagrams.

Bending moment · ULS

Whole-frame bending moment, auto-scaled to fit. Plotted on the tension face (sagging positive). Each member's largest moment is marked at its controlling section; the governing value is boxed. Values in kN·m.

Shear force · ULS

Whole-frame shear force, auto-scaled to fit. Each member's largest shear is marked at its controlling section; the governing value is boxed. Values in kN.
